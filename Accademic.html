Our Academics

We are an English-speaking school in Dschang, Cameroon, W. Africa.
We offer kindergarten (nursery school) all the way to class 7.
Each class covers the following main curriculum.

Literacy

We offer classes in English and French, We also offer civics, literature and writing to improve literacy.

Writing

Writing is a key component in all our classes. We encourage clear, thoughtful expression through writing.

Mathematics

Mathematics is core and fundamental to many disciplines, We cover mathematics (arithmetic) in all classes.

Science

Science is fundamental to society. We touch areas of science and also expose students to information technology.

Geography

Geography is fundamental to understanding how the earth works. Goegraphy is covered in all classes.

Physical Education

We ensure students remain active and understand the benefits of physical education. PE is covered in all classes.
