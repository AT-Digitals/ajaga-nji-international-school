Work completed on the Intellexi Bridge

Work has completed on the bridge connecting the town to the suburb of Tchale. This allows for full restoration of transportation services for Ngumys International School. We want to take this opportunity to thank the municipality of Dschang for the timely construction of the bridge. It certainly looks like what has been built is here to say.

“It is a father unfortunate situation but the teachers are taking this opportunity to engage in much needly morning exercise.”
