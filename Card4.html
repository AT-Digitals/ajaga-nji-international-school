The pupils in class 1 enjoy more room

Over the summer of 2021, we expanded the square area in Class 1. Our students now have more room and space between desk. Also, this new expanded capacity facilitates interaction between the teach and pupils.

“The expanded capacity allows my assistant and I easily reach all pupils even though we have added a few additional pupils.”
