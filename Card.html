Ngumys Fantastic ends a rough year in with a bang

It was a rough here for Ngumys Fanstastic Internation School. We lost our father, dad and founder, Professor Ajaga Nji in July. Yet the staff of Ngumys with support from the Nji family rallied together to ensure the school continues operational without any hiccups. To celebrate the schools success this rough year, the staff at Ngumys hosted a successful and joyous Christmas party. It was a celebration worth capturing.

“It is a father unfortunate situation but the teachers are taking this opportunity to engage in much needly morning exercise.”
