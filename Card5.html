Gearing up for the school year 2022

It appeared to be a long summer. Not only did we lose our founding father and mentor, but it also seemed like an extra-ordinary long summer. The pupils and staff at Ngumy's we very excited to be back on campus. They look forward to the routines and educational activities that give the special experience at Ngumy's.

“The kids could not wait to come back. Everyone wanted to get right to it especially with the early morning routines which are usually very engaging.”
