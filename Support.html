Our Initiatives

OSAT

Operation Sponsor A Teacher

Help support quality education by funding passionate teachers for underprivileged students.

OSIS

Operation Sponsor Internet Services

Provide reliable internet access to students and classrooms to enhance learning opportunities.

OSEG

Operation Support Electricity & Generators

Helping maintain uninterrupted education by supporting electricity, generators, or backup power in rural classrooms.

OSTS

Operation Sponsor Transport Services

Ensuring safe and reliable transport for students and teachers to access schools regularly.

OSAC

Operation Build A Classroom (Music, etc)

Funding the construction and renovation of classrooms, including essential furniture and learning equipment.

OSCD

Operation Sponsor Community Development

Driving broader impact through infrastructure, skill training, and development initiatives for communities.
