Our Academies

Learning Begins
With Us

We, at Champion School offer supportive and inspirational environments for young enquiring minds to learn and grow with us. Our passion for learning means we achieve more than outstanding results. We strive to build confident and creative thinkers and aim at delivering an education that is truly relevant to their future.

We are an early learning academy focused on social-emotional development and early literacy and numeracy. Our students walk out with the character and confidence to make their mark in the world, equipped with the knowledge and real-world skills that take them way ahead in the industry they may serve.

Curriculum
Overview

Literacy
We offer classes in English and French. We also offer civics, literature, and writing to improve literacy.
Science
Science is fundamental to society. We touch on various areas of science and introduce information technology.
Writing
All our classes include writing components, with a strong emphasis on developing good writing skills.
Geography
Geography helps students understand how the earth works. It is taught in all our classes.
Mathematics
Mathematics is essential. We teach arithmetic and mathematical logic in all classes.
Physical Education
We ensure our students stay active and understand the value of physical fitness in all our classes.
