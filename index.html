Honoring a Legacy Through Education
We honor the enduring legacy of our beloved father, whose vision and values laid the foundation for everything we continue to build today. Though he is no longer with us, his spirit lives on in the mission to educate, empower, and uplift future generations. His dedication to nurturing young minds, fostering strong communities, and leading by example continues to inspire our work. Through each child’s success, his legacy grows — reminding us that the greatest impact we can make is in the lives we touch and the futures we help shape. In his memory, we remain committed to carrying forward his dreams with integrity, compassion, and purpose.

Gerd Nji — Chair & Administartion to Corporate Strategy
