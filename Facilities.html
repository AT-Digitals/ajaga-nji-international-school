Our Facilities

Classrooms

Our classrooms are world class and designed to ignite cognitive and creative senses while enabling effective parent-teacher interaction.

Library & Computer Center

The Afor Judith ICT center hosts hundreds of books and 30+ laptops to introduce children to computing fundamentals.

Cafeteria / Dining Room

Our cafeteria accommodates 300 people and features a fully-equipped kitchen with modern audio-visual facilities.

Sporting / Playground Complex

Our complex includes handball, volleyball, basketball, racketball, and vast open spaces for active play and exercise.

Water System

A high-capacity rainwater storage system supports critical school functions including toilets and sinks.

Well Water

Our deep-drilled well provides clean and safe drinking water for the school and the surrounding community.

Backup Electricity

Solar panel systems generate sufficient backup power to run ICT labs and other facilities during power outages.

Transportation

A fleet of 4 Toyota vans ensures reliable pick-up and drop-off services for students across designated routes.

Health & Medical

Our on-site dispensary offers basic first-aid, with full medical support from Dr. Demaze’s Clinic for staff and students.

We are a Bilingual Nursery and Elementary School in Dschang, Cameroon, W. Africa.
