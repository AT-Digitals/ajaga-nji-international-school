We pride ourselves in the fact that Ngumys is a home away from home for our students, a place where they can learn and grow both in and outside of the classroom.
Whether in the residence halls or on the sports fields, students build strong bonds with peers, learn valuable leadership lessons, and develop themselves through a wide variety of clubs, sports, and leadership roles.
